How it works

  1. Connect your wallet on Base.
  2. Pick a punk you own from the grid.
  3. Pick a BaseColor from your palette.
  4. Click any area of the punk to flood-fill it.
  5. Undo or reset if you mess up.
  6. Click Save on-chain to upload the image and write a new tokenURI. You sign the tx — no backend.

Click a grey region with a color selected to flood-fill it. Black outlines are never overwritten.
